What it does

Grades a mobile app's store listing against 28 deterministic ASO rules (0–100, per-field fixes, no AI), then generates a validated launch kit — A/B store rewrites, Product Hunt draft, 7-day social calendar, community posts and press — each re-graded by the same engine until it scores ≥ 90.

Target audience

Solo & indie mobile-app developers and small studios launching a new app who can't afford a $2–5k ASO/marketing agency.

Expected buyer countries

Global, English-first (v1): United States, United Kingdom, Canada, Australia, plus large indie-dev hubs — India, Germany/EU, Southeast Asia.

Competitors

ASO analytics suites (AppTweak, Sensor Tower, AppFollow, MobileAction) that report but don't rewrite; app-launch agencies (costly, slow); generic AI copywriters (ChatGPT/Jasper) with no store rules and no validation.

Our advantage

One deterministic 28-rule engine grades both the human and the AI — the rewrite must clear the same ≥ 90 bar or it regenerates, so the output is provably store-legal, not "AI slop." End-to-end (grade → full kit), free with no API key to grade, 100% reproducible.
